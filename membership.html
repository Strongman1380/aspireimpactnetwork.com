Membership FAQ

Can I upgrade or downgrade my membership?

Yes! You can change your membership level at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.

What if I need to cancel my membership?

You can cancel your paid membership at any time with no penalties. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

Are there discounts for nonprofits?

Yes! Qualified 501(c)(3) organizations receive a 20% discount on Premier and Pro memberships. Contact us for verification.

How do I access the training modules?

Premier and Pro members receive login credentials to our learning management system within 24 hours of membership activation.

Can organizations have multiple members?

Absolutely! We offer team pricing for organizations with 5 or more members. Contact us for custom pricing.

What's included in the quarterly strategy calls?

Pro members get 30-minute one-on-one calls with our team to discuss program development, digital strategy, or organizational challenges.
