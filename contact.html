Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to urgent family situations?

For crisis situations, we aim to respond within 2-4 hours during business hours. After hours, we have an on-call system for emergencies.

Do you work with organizations outside of Nebraska?

Absolutely! While we're based in Nebraska, our digital services are available nationwide, and we can provide remote consultation for program development.

What's included in a free consultation?

We'll assess your current situation, discuss your goals, and provide initial recommendations. For digital services, we include a basic audit of your current online presence.

Can you help with grant writing or funding applications?

Yes! Our team has experience with various funding sources and can assist with grant applications, particularly for justice and family support programs.

What makes your domestic violence program different?

We use the evidence-based Duluth Model with small class sizes (max 5) and a structured accountability system. Our 95% completion rate speaks to our program's effectiveness.

Do you offer payment plans or sliding scale fees?

We work with organizations to find solutions that fit their budget. Contact us to discuss payment options and potential discounts for qualifying nonprofits.

Our Location

Located in Geneva, Nebraska, we serve communities throughout the Midwest and beyond. While our justice support programs focus on local communities, our digital services are available nationwide.
